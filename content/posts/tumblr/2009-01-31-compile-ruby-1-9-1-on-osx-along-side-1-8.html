Compile Ruby 1.9.1 on OSX along-side 1.8

Install the first official/stable release of Ruby 1.9 (Ruby 1.9.1-p0) harmlessly alongside Ruby 1.8 on OSX. This will install the Ruby 1.9 executables as ruby19, irb19, etc so as not to harm your existing installation.
