Cucumber, Webrat, and uninitialized constant ActionController::AbstractRequest (NameError)

If you’re using Cucumber and Webrat and you run into this error: uninitialized constant ActionController::AbstractRequest (NameError) it’s likely that your version of webrat or nokogiri is out of date.

I had this problem running webrat 0.4.0 and nokogiri 1.1.1. I ran ‘sudo gem update webrat’ and it updated to 0.4.3 and it also updated it’s nokogiri dependency to 1.2.3. Updating my gems fixed the issue.

This is after searching google for 45 mins and coming up empty handed.

Lesson: Keep your gems up to date to avoid obscure failures.
