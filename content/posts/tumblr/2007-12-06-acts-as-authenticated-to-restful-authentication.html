Acts_as_authenticated to RESTful Authentication

I just finished converting an existing application with a bunch of custom methods from acts_as_authenticated to restful_authentication. It took a good portion of the day — but I managed to get it done without major complications and with full Rspec coverage. 

If you plan on doing this, it takes longer than you think - especially if you’ve customized your authentication system.
