rake svn:commit m="safe svn commits"

Safely commit to you repository with this simple rake task I whipped up.  This will display the files to be committed, update your working copy to the latest code, migrate your database, run your specs, and commit if everything is all set.  Another side effect is that you have a larger window in which to cancel your commits — which can be a good thing.
