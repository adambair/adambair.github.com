Rails Autotest with Growl in Leopard

Unfortunately growlnotify doesn’t seem to work in OSX Leopard - there is an alternative. You can use AppleScript to fire off the notifications to Growl instead of using the growlnotify command that is packaged with Growl.

It’s a one-liner change to your .autotest file which points to your shiny new AppleScript instead of growlnotify.
